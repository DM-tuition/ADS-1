About · Teenage Cancer Trust
Every day, seven young people aged 13–24 hear the words 'you have cancer'. Teenage Cancer Trust is the only UK charity dedicated to the specialised nursing care and support young people need to get through it.

100 push-ups a day. Every day. All of November. 3,000 reps each by the end — but this one was bigger than just us.

We opened it up on GymRats and 119 people joined in, logging a combined 218,197 push-ups over 30 days. We promoted it everywhere — including a live demo at Utsav — and capped it off with an awards ceremony on 7 December: Pizza Anna catering, certificates and a trophy.

The goal was £1,000 for Teenage Cancer Trust. We smashed it. Because every day, seven young people aged 13–24 hear the words 'you have cancer' — and they shouldn't have to face it alone.
