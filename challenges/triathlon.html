DISCIPLINES

A self-organised, full triathlon. No event. No crowd. No medal at the end.

2,000m in the pool — our longest swim to date, knocked out in 1:05:11 — then straight onto the bike for 40km, then 10km on dead legs to bring it home.

Three disciplines, linked together by nothing but the will to keep going. Sometimes you do it just to prove to yourself that you can.
