Who We Are

Two mates.
One mission.

AyaanDanShenanigins is a charity endurance brand built by two school friends from Manchester — Ayaan and Dan. The concept: do increasingly ridiculous physical challenges, document everything honestly, and raise real money for real causes.

Started in August 2025 and inspired by David Goggins, every challenge is bigger and more painful than the last. This isn't a fun run. It's a sufferfest — and that's the point.
